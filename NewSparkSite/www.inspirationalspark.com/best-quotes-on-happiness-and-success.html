Best Quotes on Happiness and Success in Life, in an Inspirational Video

It only takes a couple of seconds to re-inspire yourself, by means of a few words of encouragement. Below are some favorite quotes on happiness and success from an inspirational video we found that contained all of these great sayings. (We had originally posted the video itself, but it has been blocked due to its containing songs that infringed on copyrights. The quotes are still great, however, so we chose to leave our listing up for your use, while removing the video clip.You might want to jot down your favorite quotes on a post-it or slip of paper, to post it where you'll see it from time to time, as a reminder. We hope you enjoy this quick shot of inspiration from some amazing people.


Kiyosaki Quote on Taking Actions Today:

"Your future is created by what you do today, not tomorrow."
~ Robert Kiyosaki
Sharma Zen-like Saying on Self-Improvement and Avoiding the Trap of Superiority:

"There is nothing noble about being superior to some other person. True nobility lies in being superior to your former self. "
~ R. Sharma
Jim Rohn's Best Quote on Happiness and Success:

"Happiness is not something you postpone for the future; It is something you design for the present!"
~ J. Rohn
Famous Gandhi Saying on Happiness and Being in Integrity with Yourself:

"Happiness is when what you think, what you say, what you do are in harmony."
~ Mahatma Gandhi
A Funny and Wise Quote on Happiness When and Where You Least Expect It:

"Happiness often sneaks in a door you didn't think was open."
~ John Barrymore
One of the Best Quotes on Happiness and Success and Not Taking Happiness for Granted:

"Happiness always looks small while you hold it in your hands, but let it go, and you learn at once how big and precious it is."
~ Maxim Gorky
Tolstoy Quote on Happiness and Attitude - Our Happiness is Determined by Our Perceptions:

"Happiness does not depend on outward things, but on the way we see them."
~ Leo Tolstoy
Dale Carnegie on Happiness and Mental Attitude:

"Happiness doesnt depend on any external conditions, it is governed by our mental attitude."
~ Dale Carnegie
A Truly Classic Thomas Jefferson Quote on Right Mental Attitude - Very Powerful:

"Nothing can stop the man with the right mental attitude from achieving his goal; nothing on earth can help the man with the wrong mental attitude."
~ Thomas Jefferson
Famous Adage on Attitude, from Winston Churchill:

"Attitude is a little thing that makes a big difference."
~ Winston Churchill
Dr. Wayne Dyer Quote on Changing Our Lives by Changing Our Minds:

"What we think determines what happens to us, so if we want to change our lives, we need to stretch our minds."
~ Wayne Dyer
Famous Buddha Saying on the Importance of Our Thoughts - We Are What We Think:

"We are what we think. All that we are arises with our thoughts. With our thoughts, we make the world. "
~ Buddha
More from Buddha on Our Thoughts Creating Our Reality:

"We are shaped by our thoughts; we become what we think. When the mind is pure, joy follows like a shadow that never leaves."
~ Buddha
A Short, Concise Saying on Success Starting on the Inside - Positive Thinking:

"Success on the outside indeed begins with success on the inside."
~ R. Sharma
Conrad Hilton Quotation on Success Being Based on Taking Action:

"Success seems to be connected with action. Successful people keep moving. They make mistakes, but they dont quit."
~ Conrad Hilton
Wilfred Peterson Thought on Success Being Attained by Focusing Our Full Power, with Passion:

"Success is focusing the full power of all you are on what you have a burning desire to achieve."
~ Wilfred Peterson
Great Quote on Success in Life:

"There is only one success  to be able to spend your life in our own way."
~ Christopher Morley
The Classic Winston Churchill Quote on Persisting Through Multiple Failures, to Success:

"Success is going from failure to failure without a loss of enthusiasm."
~ Winston Churchill
The Magic of Believing in Yourself Quote from Goethe:

"Magic is believing in yourself. If you can do that, you can make anything happen."
~ Johann Wolfgang von Goethe
