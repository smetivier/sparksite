Letting Go Quotes - Moving On with Life Quotes

Letting go of past mistakes, unfortunate events, and past hurts can be difficult. Hopefully, one of these letting go quotes will help you to move past the pain or former circumstances, into your future life. It only takes a couple of seconds to re-inspire yourself. Get a quick shot of inspiration via these short inspirational quotes from some amazing people, such as: Oprah Winfrey, Alexander Graham Bell, and Richard Carlson.


Letting Go Quotes

Famous Letting Go Saying from Lao Tzu:

"When I let go of what I am, I become what I might be."
~ Lao Tzu
Herman Hesse Quote on Letting Go:

"Some of us think holding on makes us strong; but sometimes it is letting go."
~ Herman Hesse
Anna Quindlen Quote on Giving Up on Being Perfect:

"The thing that is really hard, and really amazing, is giving up on being perfect and beginning the work of becoming yourself."
~ Anna Quindlen
Classic Oprah Winfrey Quote - Let Go and Live Every Moment:

"Breathe. Let go. And remind yourself that this very moment is the only one you know you have for sure."
~ Oprah Winfrey
Alexander Graham Bell Quote on Moving On Without Regret:

"When one door closes another door opens; but we so often look so long and so regretfully upon the closed door, that we do not see the ones which open for us."
~ Alexander Graham Bell
Michael Cibenko Moving On Quote:

"One problem with gazing too frequently into the past is that we may turn around to find the future has run out on us."
~ Michael Cibenko
Letting Go of the Past Quote from Isabelle Eberhardt:

"The farther behind I leave the past, the closer I am to forging my own character."
~ Isabelle Eberhardt
Harriet Nelson Quote on Forgiving Everyone - including Yourself:

"Forgive all who have offended you, not for them, but for yourself."
~ Harriet Nelson
Booker T. Washington Letting Go Quote:

"You can't hold a man down without staying down with him."
~ Booker T. Washington
Classic and Funny Ann Landers Quote on Letting Go of Resentment:

"Hanging onto resentment is letting someone you despise live rent-free in your head."
~ Ann Landers
Joseph Campbell Moving On Quote - Letting Go of Our Former Life:

"We must be willing to let go of the life we have planned, so as to accept the life that is waiting for us."
~ Joseph Campbell
Anne Morrow Lindbergh Quote on Living in the Moment:

"If you surrender completely to the moments as they pass, you live more richly those moments."
~ Anne Morrow Lindbergh
Raymond Lindquist Quote on Letting Go of the Familiar:

"Courage is the power to let go of the familiar."
~ Raymond Lindquist
Great Quote on Learning to Let Go, from Steve Maraboli:

"You must learn to let go. Release the stress. You were never in control anyway."
~ Steve Maraboli
Famous Richard Carlson Quote on Letting Go:

"Ask yourself this question: 'Will this matter a year from now?'"
~ Richard Carlson Ph.D
