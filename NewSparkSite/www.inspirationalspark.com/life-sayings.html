Life Sayings and Quotes About Living Life

We've selected the very best life sayings and positive life quotes from a huge number of sources. Here are the greatest quotes on living life, from some of the most inspirational people who have ever lived, including Confucius, Ralph Waldo Emerson, Oscar Wilde, and Mark Twain.



Albert Camus Living Life Quote:

"Live to the point of tears."
~ Albert Camus
Betty Smith Inspiring Life Quote:

"Look at everything as though you were seeing it either for the first or last time. Then your time on earth will be filled with glory. "
~ Betty Smith
Robert Heinlein Life Saying:

"To enjoy the flavor of life take big bites."
~ Robert Heinlein
Inspiring Life Quote by Horace:

"Carpe Diem! Rejoice while you are alive; enjoy the day; live life to the fullest; make the most of what you have. It is later than you think. "
~ Horace
Jacques Torres Life Saying:

"Life is short. Eat dessert first."
~ Jacques Torres
Confucius Positive Life Quote:

"Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life. "
~ Confucius

Eleanor Roosevelt Inspiring Life Quote:

"The purpose of life is to live it, to taste experience to the utmost, to reach out eagerly and without fear for newer and richer experience."
~ Eleanor Roosevelt
Jonathan Swift Life Saying:

"May you live all the days of your life. "
~ Jonathan Swift
Mathew Arnold Living Life Quote:

"Life is not having and getting, but being and becoming."
~ Mathew Arnold
Jack London Inspiring Life Quote:

"I would rather be ashes than dust! I would rather that my spark should burn out in a brilliant blaze than it should be stifled by dry rot. I would rather be a superb meteor, every atom of me in magnificent glow, than a sleepy and permanent planet. The proper function of man is to live, not to exist. I shall not waste my days in trying to prolong them. I shall use my time."
~ Jack London
Ralph Waldo Emerson Positive Life Quote:

"It is not length of life, but depth of life."
~ Ralph Waldo Emerson
Ferris Bueller Positive Life Quote:

"Life moves pretty fast. If you don't stop and look around once in a while, you could miss it. "
~ Ferris Bueller
