My Life Quotes - Quotations from Famous Thinkers on Their Lives

It only takes a couple of seconds to re-inspire yourself. Get a quick shot of inspiration via these my life quotes from some amazing people, such as: Henry David Thoreau, Albert Camus, and Montaigne.


End of My Life Quote from Erma Bombeck:

"When I stand before God at the end of my life, I would hope that I would not have a single bit of talent left, and could say, 'I used everything you gave me.'"
~ Erma Bombeck
My Life's Journey Quote:

"Shall I redirect my life's journey because down some side road might be some trifle I'm entitled to?"
~ Robert Brault
Famous My Life of Terrible Misfortunes Quote:

"My life has been full of terrible misfortunes most of which never happened."
~ Michel de Montaigne
Funny Quote about My Life and What I Wanted to Be:

"All my life, I always wanted to be somebody. Now I see that I should have been more specific."
~ Jane Wagner (sometimes credited to Lily Tomlin)
Living Your Own Life Quote:

"All my life I had been looking for something, and everywhere I turned someone tried to tell me what it was. I accepted their answers too, though they were often in contradiction and even self-contradictory. I was na√Øve. I was looking for myself and asking everyone except myself questions which I, and only I, could answer. It took me a long time and much painful boomeranging of my expectations to achieve a realization everyone else appears to have been born with: that I am nobody but myself."
~ Ralph Ellison
Living in the Present Quote:

"Having spent the better part of my life trying either to relive the past or experience the future before it arrives, I have come to believe that in between these two extremes is peace."
~ Unknown
Famous Living for Today Quote from D'Souza:

"For a long time it had seemed to me that life was about to begin - real life. But there was always some obstacle in the way. Something to be got through first, some unfinished business, time still to be served, a debt to be paid. Then life would begin. At last it dawned on me that these obstacles were my life. "
~ Alfred D'Souza
Albert Camus Quote on Living a Life of Belief:

"I would rather live my life as if there is a God and die to find out there isn't, than live my life as if there isn't and die to find out there is."
~ Albert Camus
Funny and True My Life Quote:

"Everything I did in my life that was worthwhile, I caught hell for."
~ Earl Warren, Supreme Court Justice
Walt Disney Quote about Life:

"I have been up against tough competition all my life. I wouldn't know how to get along without it."
~ Walt Disney
Michael Jordan Quote on Pushing Through Failure to Success:

"I've failed over and over and over again in my life and that is why I succeed. "
~ Michael Jordan
Surprising Georgia O'Keeffe Quote on Pushing Past Fear in Life:

"I've been absolutely terrified every moment of my life - and I've never let it keep me from doing a single thing I wanted to do."
~ Georgia O'Keeffe, renowned photographer
