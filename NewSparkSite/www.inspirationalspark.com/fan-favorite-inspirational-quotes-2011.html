Our Facebook Fans' Favorite Inspirational Quotes of the Year 2011

We've been publishing inspirational quotes on our Facebook page for over a year, and are fans have identified their favorites by commenting and sharing on them. These are our Facebook Fans' favorite inspirational quotes for 2011, determined by our counting the number of likes, comments, and shares for all of our quote posts. So, in case you've only joined us recently, or could use a refresh, or are just looking for the best of the best inspirational quotes, here they are!


Our Fans' #1 Favorite Inspirational Quote, from William Jennings Bryan:

"Destiny is not a matter of chance; it is a matter of choice. It is not a thing to be waited for; it is a thing to be achieved."
~ William Jennings Bryan
Our Fans' #2 Favorite Inspirational Quote, from Mother Teresa - a True Classic:

"People are often unreasonable and self-centered. Forgive them anyway. If you are kind, people may accuse you of ulterior motives. Be kind anyway. If you are honest, people may cheat you. Be honest anyway. If you find happiness, people may be jealous. Be happy anyway. The good you do today may be forgotten tomorrow. Do good anyway. Give the world the best you have and it may never be enough. Give your best anyway. For you see, in the end, it is between you and God. It was never between you and them anyway."
~ Mother Teresa
Fan Favorite #3, from Ann Landers On How To Treat Others:

"The true measure of a man is how he treats someone who can do him absolutely no good."
~ Ann Landers
Favorite Quote #4, from Harriet Nelson - Great Advice on Forgiveness:

"Forgive all who have offended you, not for them, but for yourself."
~ Harriet Nelson
#5 Fan Favorite from Marilyn Monroe:

"I believe that everything happens for a reason. People change so that you can learn to let go, things go wrong so that you appreciate them when they're right, you believe lies so you eventually learn to trust no one but yourself, and sometimes good things fall apart so better things can fall together."
~ Marilyn Monroe
#6 Favorite Quote of 2011, from the Buddha:

"All that we are is the result of What we have thought. The mind is everything. What we think, we become."
~ Buddha
#7 Fan Favorite, Great Advice on Appreciating the Little Things, by Robert Brault:

"Enjoy the little things, for one day you may look back and realize they were the big things."
~ Robert Brault
#8 Fan Favorite Quote, On Becoming, from e. e. cummings:

"It takes courage to grow up and become who you really are."
~ e. e. cummings
# 9 Fan Favorite Quote of 2011, on Loving Deeply, from Lao Tzu:

"Being deeply loved by someone gives you strength, while loving someone deeply gives you courage."
~ Lao Tzu
#10 Favorite Quote of 2011, from Taryn Rose:

"Fear regret more than failure."
~ Taryn Rose
