Inspirational Quotes About Life - Inspirational Sayings About Life

Given the right inspirational quote about life, you can re-inspire yourself in a flash. Get a quick shot of inspiration via these these inspirational quotes about life, from some truly amazing people, such as: Henry David Thoreau, Vincent van Gogh, and Buddha.


Inspirational Quote about Life from Buddha:

"Your work is to discover your work and then with all your heart to give yourself to it."
~ Buddha
Denis Waitley Finding Your Passion Quote:

"Chase your passion, not your pension."
~ Denis Waitley
Inspirational Quote on Life - the Importance of Giving Your Best Effort, Above All::

"The important thing in life is not the victory but the contest; the essential thing is not to have won but to have fought well."
~ Unknown
Great Quote About Living Life to the Fullest:

"Laugh when you can, apologize when you should, and let go of what you can't change. Kiss slowly, play hard, forgive quickly, take chances, give everything and have no regrets. Life's too short to be anythingbut happy."
~ Unknown
Great Quote On Going For It, from Zig Ziglar:

"Will you look back on life and say, 'I wish I had,' or 'Im glad I did'?"
~ Zig Ziglar
Henry David Thoreau Quote on Spending Time Wisely:

"The price of anything is the amount of life you exchange for it."
~ Henry David Thoreau
Inspirational Quote on Passion in Life:

"Your profession is not what brings home your paycheck. Your profession is what you were put on earth to do. With such passion and such intensity that it becomes spiritual in calling."
~ Vincent Van Gogh
Inspirational Quote about Life and Purpose, by Billy Mills:

"Your life is a gift from the Creator. Your gift back to the Creator is what you do with your life."
~ Billy Mills
Woodrow Wilson Quote on Your Life's Purpose:

"We are not here merely to make a living. We are here to enrich the world."
~ Woodrow Wilson
Robert Brault Quote about Life and Attitude:

"Life is like sailing. You can use any wind to go in any direction."
~ Robert Brault
Inspiring Quote about Life and Love:

"Those who love deeply never grow old; they may die of old age, but they die young."
~ A. W. Pinero
Inspirational Quote about Life and Living Life to the Fullest:

"Dont be afraid your life will end. Be afraid that it will never begin."
~ Grace Hansen
Bob Marley Quote on Living a Great Life:

"The greatness of a man is not in how much wealth he acquires, but in his integrity and his ability to affect those around him positively."
~ Bob Marley
Inspiring Quote on Imagination, and One's Outlook on Life:

"I think the world really boils down to two types of people  those who see shapes in cloud formations, and those who just see clouds."
~ Danzae Pace
Classic Grandma Moses Quote about Life:

"Life is what we make it, always has been, always will be."
~ Grandma Moses
